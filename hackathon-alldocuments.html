About Us

The Green Horizon (TGH) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to worldwide environmental education with the goal of protecting a flourishing ecosystem and safeguarding the environmental rights of future generations.

In recent years TGH held a series of public events targeting youth and promoting environmental awareness. At these events TGH focused on integrating environmental awareness with literature and the arts using tools such as children’s art exhibitions, large scale environmental poetry readings, environmental science contests, waste materials recycling, and “Change the World from Me” initiatives. Our continuing efforts promote green culture and communities. We actively explore positive ways to encourage civic participation in environmental protection by:

•Educating and inspiring youth to explore the beauty of nature and diversity of life

•Raising awareness about the urgent need for environmental protection in children and youths in the hope that they will influence their elders
